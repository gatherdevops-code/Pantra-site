Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pantra. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care and transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our food waste prevention app and services.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with our pantry tracking and food waste prevention services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Receipt Images: Photos of grocery receipts that you scan using our OCR technology
  • Pantry Data: Food items, expiration dates, and inventory information extracted from receipts or manually entered
  • Household Information: Details about household members you share your pantry with
  • Notification Preferences: Your choices for expiration reminders and personalized alerts
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our app, including features used and frequency of access
  • Device Information: Mobile device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to power your personal food waste prevention plan. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Process and extract item information from receipt images using OCR technology
  • Track food expiration dates and send you timely reminders
  • Generate AI-powered pantry suggestions and meal recommendations
  • Enable household sharing and synchronization across devices
  • Calculate your cost savings and environmental impact
  • Improve our app's accuracy and performance
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

4. Our Data Privacy Commitment

We never sell your data. Your receipt images, pantry inventory, and personal information are yours alone. We do not:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your receipt images with advertisers or data brokers
  • Use your pantry information for marketing purposes outside of Pantra
  • Provide your shopping habits to grocery stores or other retailers

5. How We Store Receipt Images

Receipt images are stored securely using industry-standard encryption (AES-256). Images are processed using OCR technology to extract item information, then stored on secure cloud servers with restricted access. You can delete your receipt images at any time through the app settings.

6. Data Sharing and Household Features

When you use our household sharing feature, you explicitly choose which family members can view your shared pantry inventory. Only the household members you invite will have access to this information. We do not share your data with anyone outside your designated household.

7. Notifications and Communications

We send you personalized expiration reminders and pantry suggestions based on your inventory. You can customize notification preferences or opt out entirely through your app settings. We may also send important service updates about your account.

8. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission, secure server storage, regular security audits, and access controls limiting who can view your data internally.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion, we will permanently remove your receipt images, pantry data, and personal information within 30 days.

10. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and receipt images
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your pantry data in a portable format
  • Opt out of personalized suggestions and notifications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

11. Children's Privacy

Pantra is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or app notification and update the "Last Updated" date above.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at privacy@pantra.com.
